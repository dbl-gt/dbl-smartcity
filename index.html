  •   About
    • DBL SmartCity

        DBL SmartCity

        An information processing platform developed at the Digital Building Laboratory (DBL), Georgia Institute of Technology. The platform allows for storing, processing, integrating, retrieving, and visualizing of large and complex sets of data collected from a variety of geo-located sources collocated within human settlements at various scales, such as metropolitan areas, cities, towns, and suburban areas.





        Keywords: SmartCity • Internet of Things (IoT) • Building Information Model (BIM) • Big Data
